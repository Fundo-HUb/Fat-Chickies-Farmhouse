Our Mission

At Fat Chickies farmhouse, our mission is to cultivate a sustainable, ethical ecosystem that nurtures chickens from hatchling to harvest. We are committed to providing high-quality eggs, nutritious meat, and wholesome chicks while prioritizing animal welfare and environmental stewardship. By embracing innovative farming practices and fostering a strong connection with our community, we aim to inspire a deeper appreciation for the journey of food from farm to table.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leader in regenerative agriculture, setting the standard for chicken farming that harmonizes productivity with ecological responsibility. We envision a future where our farm serves as a model of sustainable practices, educating consumers about the importance of conscious choices in food production. By empowering local communities through farm tours, workshops, and partnerships, we strive to create a thriving ecosystem that nurtures both the land and the people who depend on it. Together, we will redefine the role of farming in a rapidly changing world, ensuring a vibrant, healthy future for generations to come.
